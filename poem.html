I write of you, though words may fail,
A mind so bright, a soul so hale.
Genuine, lovely, with grace untold,
A smile that turns the dull to gold.
Each time I speak, nerves take hold,
Yet patience in you is quiet and bold.
You laugh it off, you never mind,
A kindness rare, a heart so kind.
I fumble, I fall, I learn, I rise,
A journey traced in uncertain skies.
Yet in your words, I found my way,
A light that led me past my fray.
Destiny unfolds with each new stride,
Not a place to reach, but a flame inside.
So here's to the journey, the dream, the climb,
A belated wish, but pure and prime.
Happy birthday to the one so free,
Who taught me to walk the path of me.
